About

Hello! I am Bilal Dastagir. Applied Blockchain, Applied Cryptography Engineer, Zero-Knowledge Cryptography Engineer, HW/SW Co-Design Engineer and AI Researcher

As a Blockchain Architect, my objective is to design and develop a trusted data platform that securely aggregates and standardizes user data through blockchain-based technology. With extensive experience in blockchain-related development and web3 technologies, I will leverage my skills in smart contract deployment, research, and system architecture to design and implement efficient and effective solutions.

As an Applied Cryptography Engineer, my objective is to apply my expertise and experience in developing and implementing cryptographic solutions to encrypt and secure user data on your platform. I aim to contribute to the development of robust and secure cryptographic solutions that protect user data from vulnerabilities and threats.

As a Zero-Knowledge Cryptography Engineer, I aim to leverage my 5+ years of engineering experience and strong cryptography and zero-knowledge proofs expertise, along with my proficiency in Golang, Rust, or C/C++, to develop and optimize ZK solutions for enabling secure and private communication between the core Web3 platform and enterprise clients.

As an HW/SW Co-Design Engineer, my objective is to leverage my skills and experience in hardware and software development to create optimized solutions for your organization. With a background in Electrical Engineering and extensive experience in programming languages such as C/C++, Verilog, and VHDL, I am well-equipped to design and implement complex hardware and software systems. I possess expertise in FPGA and ASIC design, and I can leverage these skills to create innovative solutions that meet the requirements of your organization. Additionally, my experience in embedded systems, sensors, and IoT, coupled with my strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail, make me an ideal candidate for this role.

As an AI Edge Researcher, I aim to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems by utilizing my expertise in AI and edge computing. My skills in machine learning algorithms, programming languages, and deep learning frameworks enable me to develop optimized models for deployment on resource-constrained edge devices. With my experience in edge hardware, time-series classification and Indoor Positioning and Indoor Navigation, I can design and implement efficient solutions that leverage the capabilities of these devices. My analytical and problem-solving skills, along with my effective communication skills, make me a valuable asset to any team that is passionate about driving innovation in AI and edge computing.
